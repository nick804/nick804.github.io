Rosewood Lodging

ABOUT US

Travel with the comforts of home...

Situated in scenic West Gardiner, Maine sits Rosewood Lodging. We provide a warm friendly environment for traveling nurses from across the U.S. to stay for short to long term housing. With a beautiful community kitchen, and a handmade high-top table crafted from local woods; every resident has a room with beautiful cedar trim, and all the amenities needed to relax after a day from work. We have a variety of rooms available, several include their own half-bath, and vanity, whilst others forgo this specific feature for a shared restroom - for a lower price, of course.

We aim to provide quality living, and a beautiful, quite environment to retreat to when you've had a stressful day at the office. When you join our environment, we'll greet you with a smile and a welcome bag with some local guides, menus, and - of course - an item to remember us by!

We are available on where you can contact us, and see if we have a wonderful room for you. Don't forget to stop by our Gallery to see some pictures of the rooms, and the environment (Especially our local crafted high-top table made from 100 year old oak!)!
