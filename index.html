Welcome to Rosewood Lodging,

Our Small Country Inn in West Gardiner, ME

Travel with the comforts of home

Rosewood Lodging, a 5-star small country Inn offers fully furnished and very comfortable rooms & studio units in a quiet environment, for healthcare professionals and others on assignment in our region.

Conveniently located in West Gardiner, Maine between 5 major medical centers within 16 to 31 miles. Easy access to major highways makes Rosewood Lodging the perfect destination to call home while you are in our region. Your comfort and peace of mind is our priority.
