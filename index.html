Rosewood Lodging

ABOUT US

Travel with the comforts of home...

Nestled in the small town of West Gardiner, Maine.

Rosewood Lodging offers very comfortable and fully furnished rooms, for short and extended stay, for travelling healthcare professionals. Amenities include smart TV's, hard wired Ethernet connections & WiFi, private 1⁄2 baths, a laundry room, and a fully-equipped open kitchen is available for those who enjoy cooking themselves or choose one of our local grocers who prepare home cooked meals.

Rosewood Lodging is conveniently located between 5 major medical centers within 16 to 31 miles. Easy access to major highways makes Rosewood Lodging the perfect destination to call home while you are on your assignment.

Our Mission

Here at Rosewood Lodging we believe in bringing high quality affordable housing to traveling healthcare professionals, for both short and extended stays, within our region. From our spacious commons and furnished rooms, to our bio-metric keypads, your comfort and peace of mind is our priority here at Rosewood. We believe in the work you do and understand the risks that you undertake, and we thank you. We have been touched by the world of medicine, and we want to give back to a community that has done so much for us.

Accomodations

Rosewood Lodging offers very comfortable rooms, Several of which have private ½ baths. There are several private shower rooms throughout the facility. We provide linens including bedding, bath towels, hand towels, wash cloths. WIFI, hard wired Ethernet connections and Roku televisions all included in your bundle.

  • Free High-speed WiFi & hard-wired ethernet connections
  • Full-service kitchen
  • Private 1/2 baths *
  • Sound-proofed walls
  • Linens, including bedding, bath towels, hand towels, and wash cloths.
  • 32-inch Roku televisions
  • Private shower rooms
  • Laundry on-premise
* Private 1/2 baths not available in every room, please contact us to find out if we have a room available with one if interested.

Dining Options

Rosewood Lodging offers a fully equipped self serve kitchen with plenty of seating. Close by is our local Grocer who offers a dine in option and a weekly menu for healthy prepared meals. In addition our local convenience store is just down the street.

In addition, we have several restaraunts within a 15-minute drive.

Rates & Fees

Rosewood Lodging offers 30 day stays or longer starting at $700.00 with a lease agreement, $100 security deposit, and a $64.00 cleaning fee (non refundable).

*For lodging less than 30 days other rates apply. Please contact us for more information.

Ways You Can Pay

We accept Cash, Certified Banks checks, and Venmo.
